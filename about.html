Frame Psychology Consultant

Human Capital is the primary emphasis of Frame Psychology Consultant, a field development committed in business. Frame was established to support stakeholders manage activity for the best possible Human Capital Management, to fulfill the demands of competitive businesses and organizations, and to enhance the integrity of professional human resources (HR).

Frame Consultant, providing psychological services ranging from psychological testing, outbound psychology, employee recruitment and selection, carrier assessment, talent assessment, assessment center, executive selection (head hunter), seminars, workshops, and training to interest, aptitude, and educational major tests.

History

  • 2019

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Starting from a college assignment, Frame Psychology Consultant was founded. All components needed to develop all psychology services started this year

  • 2019

    An Agency is Born

    In the same year, Frame Psychology Consultant registered with the Ministry of Law and Human Rights as a legal entity with holding company named Lubuk Bingkai Konsultan

  • 2020

    Transition to Full Service

    As a psychology institution, Frame Psychology has begun to open collaborations and associations with psychologists in Indonesia in developing Human Resource Empowerment services in Indonesia to meet company demands.

  • 2022

    Phase Two Expansion

    By being registered with the Indonesian Psychological Association (HIMPSI), Frame Psychology has officially transformed into a psychology institution with a focus on Organizational Industrial Psychology.
